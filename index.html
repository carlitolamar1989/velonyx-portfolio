Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build my system? +

Starter systems are delivered in 5-7 business days, Growth systems in 10-14 business days, Premium systems in 14-21 business days, and Enterprise engagements follow a custom timeline defined in the Statement of Work. The timeline starts when your deposit is received and you provide your assets (logo, photos, content). Delays in providing assets may extend the timeline.

What if I don't like the design? +

Every package includes a capped set of revision rounds within a time-boxed revision window: 2 rounds / 1 week for Starter, 3 rounds / 2 weeks for Growth, 3 rounds / 3 weeks for Premium, and 5 rounds / 4 weeks for Enterprise (custom per SOW). After the window closes or the round limit is reached, additional revisions are billed at $100/hour. We send you a live preview link before anything goes public so you can review each round carefully.

Do I own the system after it's built? +

Yes. Once you pay in full, you own the entire system — code, design, domain, customer data, all of it. You can modify it, transfer it, or do whatever you want with it. Unlike Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, or Wix, your platform is yours. Stop renting your business tools. Start owning your infrastructure.

Can my customers pay through my system? +

Yes. Every system includes Stripe payment integration so your customers can pay with credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay — on your branded payment page, via SMS link, or in person on your tablet. Growth and Premium systems also include Buy Now, Pay Later options through Klarna, Affirm, and Afterpay so your customers can split payments.

What happens after my system launches? +

Every system includes 30 days of free support after launch. Starter and Growth get standard support (3-5 business day response). Premium gets priority support (48-hour response). After 30 days, you can add a monthly Care plan — Standard ($125), Growth ($225), or Premium ($325) — to keep your system updated, secure, and supported.

Why should I choose Velonyx over SaaS tools like Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, or Jobber? +

Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, Wix, and Squarespace rent you a generic tool. You pay every month, forever, and own nothing. When they raise prices, you have no leverage. With Velonyx, you pay once for a custom-engineered system built specifically for your operation — payments, booking, customer management, SMS, branded website, all integrated — and you own it forever. Your platform, your data, your customer list, your domain. We build the whole operating system, not just a rented page.

Can I pay in installments? +

Yes. You can pay a 50% deposit to start and the remaining 50% at launch. You can also pay in full upfront and receive a 10% discount. Financing through Klarna, Affirm, or Afterpay is also available on eligible packages.

Do you work with businesses outside of my area? +

Absolutely. Everything we do is remote. We work with clients across the United States. Consultations are done over video call or phone, and your site is delivered digitally. Location doesn't matter.
