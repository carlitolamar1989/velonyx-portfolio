Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is Velonyx only for home service businesses? +

No. Velonyx engineers custom digital systems for any ambitious business — we just specialize in home service operators (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage doors) because that's where the platform hits the deepest pain. Custom builds are available for businesses in any industry ready to scale, brand out, and own their infrastructure.

How long does it take to build my system? +

Most Founding Member systems launch in 7–14 business days from when your deposit clears and you provide your assets (logo, photos, content). The timeline starts when your deposit is received and you provide your assets. Delays in providing assets may extend the timeline. Industry norm is 4–6 weeks — we move faster because we run a refined process with a small team.

What if I don't like the design? +

Every Founding Member build includes a capped set of revision rounds within a defined revision window. You get a live preview link before anything goes public — every detail reviewable, every round documented. After the revision window closes, additional changes are billed at $100/hour. Most builds reach final approval within two rounds.

Do I own the system after it's built? +

Yes — fully. Once payment is complete, you own the entire platform. The code is yours. The design is yours. The domain is yours. The customer data is yours. You can modify it, migrate it, sell it, or hand it to your kids. This is the core promise of Velonyx — you build a business asset, not a monthly bill.

Can my customers pay through my system? +

Yes. Every Velonyx build includes Stripe payment integration so your customers can pay with credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay — on your branded payment page, via SMS link, or in person on your tablet. Buy Now, Pay Later financing through Klarna, Affirm, and Afterpay is built in so customers can split payments on bigger jobs and you still get paid in full upfront.

What happens after my system launches? +

Every system includes 30 days of free support after launch. After that, the $100/month Care plan keeps your platform live, secure, and improving — hosting, uptime monitoring, security patches, content updates, SMS and payment integrations, and direct access to the founder. The first month is free for Founding Members.

Why should I choose Velonyx over SaaS tools like Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, or Jobber? +

SaaS tools like Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, Jobber, Wix, and Squarespace are rented software. You pay monthly. You build their business with your payments. You own nothing at the end. When they raise prices, you have no leverage.

Velonyx is a custom-engineered platform built specifically for your operation. One payment to build. A flat $100/month to keep it running. You own the code, the design, the customer data, the domain — everything. After three years, you've paid less than the rented stack and you walk away with an asset that lives on your balance sheet.

The right question isn't "which software is better?" It's "do you want to rent forever, or own?"

Can I pay in installments? +

Yes. You can pay a 50% deposit to start and the remaining 50% at launch, or pay in full upfront. Financing through Klarna, Affirm, or Afterpay is also available at checkout.

Do you work with home service operators outside my area? +

Absolutely. Everything we do is remote. We work with home service operators across the United States — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage doors, and any trade ready to scale. Consultations are done over video call or phone, and your platform is delivered digitally. Location doesn't matter.
