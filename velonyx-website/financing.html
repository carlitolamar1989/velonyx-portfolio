Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about financing your website.

Does checking my rate hurt my credit score? ▾

No. Pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry, which has zero impact on your credit score. A hard pull only happens if you choose to formally accept a loan offer from a specific lender.

How much can I borrow? ▾

Loan amounts range from $1,000 to $15,000 through our network. Final loan amounts are subject to lender approval based on your credit profile and income.

What credit score do I need to qualify? ▾

Our network includes lenders who work with all credit profiles — from excellent (720+) down to building credit (580+). The lower your score, the higher the rate, but offers are still available for most applicants.

How long does approval take? ▾

Pre-qualification results come back within 60 seconds for most applicants. Full approval and funding typically happens within 1–3 business days after you select a lender and complete their application.

When does my website project start? ▾

The moment your loan is funded and payment clears, your project kicks off within 24 hours. We don't make you wait — we start building as soon as you're funded.

Can I pay off the loan early? ▾

Most lenders in our network allow early payoff with no prepayment penalties. Always confirm with your specific lender at the time of offer acceptance.
