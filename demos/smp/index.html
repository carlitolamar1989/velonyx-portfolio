What SMP Is

A precision tattoo technique — for every hairline.

Scalp micropigmentation is the artistry of depositing specialized pigment into the scalp at the depth of a natural hair follicle. Under a magnifier, every dot is placed by hand.

For clients with full hair loss: we reconstruct an entire hairline and fill the scalp with thousands of natural-looking follicle replicas — undetectable even close-up.

For clients with thinning hair: we tattoo pigment into the gaps between existing hairs — known as density work — so the hair reads visibly thicker without transplants, drugs, or downtime.
