Spring Replacement

From $285 (single torsion) · From $385 (dual torsion)

Springs are the workhorses of any garage door — they balance the weight so the opener doesn't have to. When they break, your door becomes a 200-pound deadweight. We replace them with high-cycle springs rated for 25,000+ cycles (most stock springs are rated for 10,000).

Job time: 60-90 minutes. We always recommend replacing both springs at the same time — they wear together, they should retire together. You'll save a service call later.
