Garage Door Repair in Paradise, NV

Strip-adjacent service. Older homes, busy schedules. We come on your hour.

Paradise covers some of Las Vegas's oldest and most storied real estate — from the post-war ranches near UNLV, to the mid-century homes off Russell Rd and Tropicana, to the high-rise condo towers along The Strip with shared garage decks. We've worked them all.

Most of our Paradise calls fall into one of three buckets. Aging openers — half the homes here still have a 1990s-era Genie or Sears Craftsman that's reaching end-of-life; we replace with modern LiftMaster Wi-Fi models in under 2 hours. Sun-rotted seals and bottom panels — Paradise homes face direct south sun and the bottom 18" of every garage door takes brutal punishment; we swap panels in a morning. Tenant-call emergency repair — a lot of Paradise is rental property; we coordinate directly with property managers via SMS, send before/after photos, and bill back to the owner.

If you're in Paradise, we're 15-20 minutes from your driveway any time of day. Call (702) 555-0184 for same-day service.
