Garage Door Repair in Las Vegas (Central), NV

Downtown, Arts District, Historic Westside. Mid-century homes our specialty.

Central Las Vegas is the city's original DNA. From the bungalow blocks east of Charleston Blvd, to the Historic Westside and Berkley Square, to the bohemian streets of the Arts District, to the post-war ranches near UNLV's east edge — these are some of the oldest residential neighborhoods in the valley, many built between 1945 and 1965.

That means we work on a lot of narrow single-car doors here that aren't standard sizes. We carry custom torsion springs sized for non-standard 8-foot openings, replacement hardware for 1950s-60s Wayne-Dalton and Stanley original installs, and we know how to retrofit a quiet modern opener into a 70-year-old garage with low ceilings and exposed framing.

For the Arts District lofts and converted-warehouse residential conversions, we install commercial roll-up doors that double as front doors when raised — a Vegas-specific design quirk. We also handle a lot of preservation-friendly door replacements for owners restoring mid-century homes who want the look of the original but the performance of modern hardware. Same-day for residential. (702) 555-0184.
