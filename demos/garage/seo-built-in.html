What we build into every site

All four of these come standard. Most web designers either skip them or charge extra. We treat them as the floor.

1 · How Google Sees You

Why customers find you, not your competitor.

  • Each page has its own custom search listing
    When someone Googles "spring replacement Henderson," the right page on your site comes up — not just your homepage.
  • Clean, professional web addresses
    No random "?page=4837" junk in your URLs. Just /services/spring-replacement — easy to text, easy to remember.
  • Polished link previews on social and text
    When a customer sends your link in iMessage, Facebook, or WhatsApp, it shows your logo + headline + photo — not a broken-looking gray box.
  • Apple Maps and Google Maps know exactly where you are
    Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant — they all know your address, hours, and phone number.
  • Loads fast on every phone
    Slow pages lose customers in the first 3 seconds. Yours doesn't.
2 · What Google Knows About You

The hidden info that makes Google rank you higher.

  • Google knows you're a real local business
    Hours, phone, address, services offered, payment types accepted — all delivered to Google in a format Google specifically asks for.
  • Each service has its own price tag in Google's database
    "Spring replacement from $285" shows up in Google's search results — not just on your website.
  • Your 5-star reviews show up next to your business name in Google
    "4.9 ★ — 247 reviews" appears right next to your name in search results. Customers click ranked-with-stars listings 2-3× more often.
  • Your FAQs can appear directly in Google search
    When someone Googles "how much does a garage door spring cost," your answer can pop up right at the top — before any blue links.
  • Google can show navigation crumbs in results
    Search results show "Home › Services › Spring Replacement" so customers know exactly where they'll land.
3 · Owns Every Neighborhood

A custom page for every area you serve.

  • A dedicated page for every neighborhood
    8 pages here — Henderson, Summerlin, Paradise, Spring Valley, etc. Each one is its own real Google search target.
  • ZIP codes and street names listed on every area page
    When someone Googles "garage door repair 89052," your Henderson page comes up because that ZIP is on the page.
  • Real customer reviews specific to each area
    Henderson reviews on the Henderson page. Summerlin reviews on the Summerlin page. Visitors see their neighbors recommending you.
  • Service maps embedded on every area page
    Visitors see your coverage area on a real map before they pick up the phone — eliminates "do you even come out to my area?" calls.
  • Your phone, address, and hours match across every page
    Inconsistent info confuses Google and hurts your ranking. Yours is identical on every single page.
4 · Reviews + Visibility on Autopilot

Google finds everything. Customers leave more reviews.

  • A direct map of all your pages handed to Google
    No waiting for Google to discover new pages — you give Google the list directly. Pages get indexed in days, not weeks.
  • A "rules of the road" file for search engines
    Tells Google what to look at, what to skip, and where the map of your pages lives. Standard practice on every professional site.
  • Every page reachable in 2 clicks from your homepage
    Quick navigation = better Google ranking + happier customers who find what they need.
  • Photos load fast even on slow phones
    Faster pages rank higher in Google AND convert visitors into bookings at a higher rate.
  • Auto-collects Google reviews from every paying customer
    After every paid job, the system texts the customer a quick review request. Most other shops never ask. You'll have 50+ reviews in your first year.
