# /assets/audio/

## velonyx-pitch.mp3

**Status:** Unlinked from the live site. Kept for potential repurposing.

**What it is:** A ~21-minute AI-hosted audio overview of Velonyx Systems generated via Google NotebookLM. It used to be embedded on the homepage in a "Hear The Pitch" section.

**Why it's not on the site anymore:** The "Hear The Pitch" section was removed during the May 2026 rebrand cleanup (commit `6c14b35`). The format read as off-brand for the service-industry positioning ("garage door owners aren't going to listen to a 21-minute AI podcast about your business model before clicking Pay").

**Caveat:** The audio was generated **before** the rebrand to the "premium digital systems for ambitious businesses" positioning. The script in this MP3 still references the older voice ("luxury websites," "premium digital infrastructure for entrepreneurs"). **Don't use it as-is for marketing without re-listening to confirm the language matches current positioning.**

## When you'd want to use this

- Cold-outbound audio (snippet for IG/TikTok organic, podcast intro)
- Founder content for sales calls
- A revival of the "Hear The Pitch" section if you decide audio works for the service-business audience

## How to regenerate fresh / on-brand

NotebookLM is set up under your Google account. Source materials at `/content/podcast-sources/velonyx-pitch-brief.md` (or wherever the latest pitch brief lives) can be fed back through to produce a new audio aligned with current positioning.
