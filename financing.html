Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you pay.

Do I need a Klarna, Affirm, or Afterpay account before I check out? ▾

No. You can create one during checkout in about 30 seconds, or in many cases you can proceed as a guest. None of the three require a pre-existing account to get approved.

Will applying hurt my credit score? ▾

No. All three providers use a soft credit check for the Pay-in-4 option, which has zero impact on your credit score. A hard pull only occurs if you choose a longer-term monthly financing plan through Affirm or Klarna.

How does Velonyx get paid if I'm paying in installments? ▾

The lender (Klarna, Affirm, or Afterpay) pays Velonyx the full project amount immediately. Your relationship is then with the lender — you pay them back in installments. Velonyx starts your project right away regardless of your payment schedule.

Where do I select Klarna, Affirm, or Afterpay? ▾

They appear automatically on the Stripe checkout screen after you click "Pay Now" on our checkout page. You'll see them listed alongside card payment options. Just select the one you want and follow the prompts.

What if I'm not approved? ▾

If one provider doesn't approve you, you can try another — each has slightly different approval criteria. You can also pay in full via card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Zelle. Reach out to us at (877) 317-8643 and we'll find a solution.
